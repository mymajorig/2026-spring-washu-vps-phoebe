Writing Response

Prompt: What is a ritual, practice, or routine in your life that is important for your psychological wellbeing and/or fulfillment? Why?

Answer: Reading almost everyday helps keep my natural mindset alive. It's an activity which not only educates me and takes me out of my comfort zone but also acts as a place of comfort. I often read different literary genre's. The process of mentally being transported into different worlds, and creating some of those worlds in my head, sustains my creativity which I feel is a big part of my personal makeup. Reading also helps me connect to my identity whether that be the physical aspects or aspects of my personality. In a lot of ways, it helps me validate and understand experiences I go through that I don't always understand or truly see the importance of. Sometimes, it is simply an activity I do to bring me peace in times of overwhelming turmoil or instability. Additionally, I love understanding people; this is something that I hope will be integrated in my career path. Learning about different characters through books definitely assists me in this category. Even though most of these characters are fictional, their personalities and experiences are typically inspired by those in the real world; this is especially apparent in historical fiction. Whilst currently being in the process of discovering my own identity, reading has helped me shape it and solidify it in many ways. When I read, I feel like I’m immersed in my own world which is something that I think everyone should feel during their lifetime.
