Writing Response

Prompt: What is a human-made creation that brings out the best in you? Why?

Answer: This is very simple, but I would choose a book light. In general, technology inundates our lives and I find myself encountering a lot of days where I scroll on my phone before I go to sleep. It’s difficult for me to fall asleep without engaging in a piece of media beforehand whether that be a book, a show etc. There are a lot of studies that have proven that reading before sleeping is much healthier than scrolling on your phone and interacting with your screen, so I began to develop that habit more thoroughly. However, especially during the night, I find myself sensitive to the room lights around me. Even when I am completing assignments at night, my productivity and concentration is better in darker rooms and environments. Even lamp light is too strong for me at the later hours so when it came to developing a habit of reading at night, I was struggling. A book light offers you just enough light to see what you need to see, without exposing you to everything else around you which could act as distractions in this context. Through this perfect amount of light, my already tired mind avoids being overstimulated by extra brightness and everything around me. It helps me focus on the book in front of me which, in turn, gives me room to practice and develop my creativity through the mental imagery that I experience when I read. It also rids of the factor that originally blocked me from developing healthier nightly habits, thus helping me improve not just my state of tranquility at night but also my sleep schedule as a whole. It allows me to have time to sit with my creativity and stories I love without being distracted by other factors that might take me out of the moment. This also helps improve my quality of life as I feel that everyone should reserve time to be with themselves in their own world/doing what they love.
