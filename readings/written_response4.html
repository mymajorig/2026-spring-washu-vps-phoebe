Writing Response 4

Prompt: What is a human-made creation that brings out the best in you? Why?

Something I appreciate is my deep appreciation for the little things and being enamoured with the different ways you are able to view something as. When I was younger, I was able to see the smallest thing such as a leaf and appreciate its different features and tie it back to something that I love about my own life even if it’s unrelated to the original object. Even if I would see a beat up table in a dull classroom, I would be able to not see it as a useless beat up table but a table that has the same texture as something in my favorite room. It really made me appreciate life, and be open minded, and calmingly optimistic about different things. Having an optimistic perspective would also help develop my self security and making decisions based on authenticity rather than pressure or extrinsic motivations. As someone who also loves creating art such as creative writing and graphic design, appreciating the details would also enhance my natural creative energy.
